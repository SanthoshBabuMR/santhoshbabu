Creational Design Patterns

Based on the concept of creating an object.
Eg: Constructor, Factory, Abstract, Prototype, Singleton and Builder.

Structural Design Patterns

Based on the idea of building object composition i.e. how object's data and methods are structed.
Eg: Decorator, Facade, Flyweight, Adapter and Proxy.

Behavioral Design Patterns

Based on the way objects play and work together i.e. communication
Eg: Iterator, Mediator, Observer and Visitor.
